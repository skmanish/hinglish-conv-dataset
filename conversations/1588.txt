Rohan: Hey Pooja, kya haal hai? Aaj ka mausam kitna achha hai na?
Pooja: Haan Rohan, bilkul sahi kaha! Aaj ka mausam toh dil khush kar deta hai.
Rohan: Baat toh sahi hai. Mujhe lagta hai, yeh badla hua mausam humare beech kuch aisa badla hua ahsaas le aaya hai.
Pooja: Aisa kyun keh rahe ho, Rohan?
Rohan: Pooja, jabse tum mere zindagi mein aayi ho, sab kuch badla hua hai. Tumhari muskurahat hai jaise gulabi phoolon ki mahak, aur tumhara chehra hai jaise haseen mausam ka nazara.
Pooja: Oh! Tum bhi na, shayari karne lag gaye ho ab?
Rohan: Haan, shayari ka iraada tha. Par sach kehta hoon, jab tum paas hoti ho, mausam kitna haseen lagta hai.
Pooja: Tum bhi na, baatein bana lete ho. Par sach mein, jab hum saath hote hai, har cheez kitni khubsurat dikhai deti hai.
Rohan: Pooja, kuch palon ke liye batao, kya ab bhi baarish sunsaan sadakon par chalna pasand karogi?
Pooja: Rohan, fir?! Haan, firse woh saawan ki rimjhim ki awaaz sunne ki tumhari saath mein khushiyon bhari yaadein hai. Chalenge, ek chhatri share karte hue, hathon mein hathon ko lekar?
Rohan: Pooja, ye toh romance ki baat kahi jaa rahi hai. Par mujhe toh yehi achha lagta hai, ek dusre ke sang jab saath mein umar bhar baarish mein bheegenge.
Pooja: Sach kahu toh, mujhe bhi yehi sapna hai. Tumhare saath har mausam mein, har hawa mein, har bund mein ho.
Rohan: Pooja, abhi ke liye bas ek chhatri hai, lekin zindagi bhar saath nibhane ka faisla hai. Tumhe khush rakhne ka wada karta hoon.
Pooja: Rohan, tum toh humesha achhe waade karte ho. Lekin yehi wada hai ke tum mere saath hamesha khush rahoge, aur hum dono saath mein har mod pe khushiyaan bunte rahenge.
Rohan: Pooja, yeh wada nibhana mere liye aasaan hai, kyunki tum mere saath ho. I love you, Pooja.
Pooja: Rohan, I love you too. Humara yeh mausam kabhi na khatam ho, hamesha chalne aur badalte rahne wala ho.
