Hari: Arey Ananya, tune kabhi Indian folk tales aur legends ke bare mein socha hai?
Ananya: Haan Hari, mujhe woh bohot pasand hai! Kuch favorite hai tere?
Hari: Haan, mujhe Panchatantra kahaniyaan bohot acchi lagti hai. Woh toh har bachpan mein sunte hai na?
Ananya: Bilkul! Woh kahaniyan samajhne mein maze aati hai aur lesson bhi deti hai. Waise, teri favorite kahani kaunsi hai?
Hari: Meri favorite Panchatantra kahani hai "The Lion and the Mouse." Usmein dosti ki takat dikhayi gayi hai.
Ananya: Wah, Hari! Woh kahani sach mein bahut acchi hai. Suna hai South India mein kuch famous legends hain. Tujhe unke bare mein pata hai?
Hari: Haan Ananya, south India ke legend ke baare mein thoda bahut pata hai. Waise, tu bata, kis legend ke bare mein janna chahti hai?
Ananya: Mujhe Rashmi Rathi ke bare mein suna hai, woh kaafi romantic legend hai. Tu bata, woh kaisa hai?
Hari: Rashmi Rathi toh ek pyaari prem kahani hai! Woh Maharaja Bhoj ke bete Ratan Sen ke pyaar ki kahani hai. Dono alag cast ke the, par prem mein aapatti nahi li gayi.
Ananya: Sach mein? Woh bohot acchi kahani lagti hai. Prem mein koi boundaries nahi honi chahiye na? Lekin abhi bhi paraye cast se pyaar ki baat ko kafi log samajh nahi paate.
Hari: Haan Ananya, sahi kaha! Lekin samay ke saath log change hote hai. Aur pyaar sabhi ko bind kar deta hai.
Ananya: True, Hari! Pyaar ka power aur samaj ke soch badal sakta hai. Lekin, mujhe lagta hai ab hum apne pyaar ki kahani mei hi kho gaye hai.
Hari: Ananya, tu sahi kehti hai. Humari kahani bhi ek romantic legend ban sakti hai. Kya hum ek dusre ke liye Rashmi Rathi ban sakte hai?
Ananya: Hari, tujhse behtar koi Rashmi Rathi na ho sakta! Main bhi tere liye hamesha ki tarah Mahashay ki beti ho sakti hoon.
Hari: Arey, Ananya! Tu aisi baatein karti hai, mujhe sab bhul jaata hai. 
Ananya: Tabhi toh prem kuch khaas hai, Hari. Aur main chahti hoon ki humari kahani kabhi khatam na ho.
Hari: Ananya, hum dono ki kahani abhi shuru hui hai, aur kabhi khatam nahi hogi. Hum dono saath rahenge, har kadam pe, har chunauti mein!
Ananya: Hari, tere saath har pal bitana mere liye sapna hai. Tujhse milke mujhe jeevan ki saari khushiyan mil gayi hai.
Hari: Ananya, tu mere sapno ki Rajkumari hai. Aur ab hum dono apne khwabon ko haqiqat banayenge!
Ananya: Aaj se hum dono ek ho gaye, ek dusre ka saath hamesha ke liye. I love you, Hari.
Hari: I love you too, Ananya. Aaj se humari kahani aur bhi khoobsurat hogayi hai.
