Rahul: Kya haal hai, Pooja? Kuch yaad aaya Indian freedom fighters ke baare mein?
Pooja: Haan, Rahul! Aaj humare mahapurushon ke baare mein baat karte hain. Bhagat Singh ke baare mein tumhe kya pata hai?
Rahul: Bhagat Singh toh desh bhakti ki misaal hai! Unka jazba, unki veerta, aur shaheed hone ka andaz, sabne hamein garv hai.
Pooja: Haan, bilkul! Unhone British sarkar ke khilaf apne jaan ko qurbaan kar diya. Unki saahas aur himmat hamesha yaad rakhni chahiye.
Rahul: Aur tumhe Rani Lakshmibai ke baare mein kya pata hai? Jhansi ki Rani, unka naam toh aapke dil mein bhi hoga!
Pooja: Rahul, Rani Lakshmibai toh bahut hi prerna ki murti hai! Unki ladai azadi ke liye humesha yaad ki jaayegi. Unka josh, unki tyaag, woh toh humesha humein motivate karti rahegi.
Rahul: Bilkul sahi kaha, Pooja! Aur humare Rashtrapita Mahatma Gandhi ki baat karein toh unki ahinsa ke siddhant aur satyagraha ko toh kabhi bhool nahi sakte.
Pooja: Sahi kaha, Rahul! Gandhi ji ka yugdaan desh ko ekta aur azaadi ki disha mein agge badhaya. Unhone hamare samaj ko satya aur shanti ka marg dikhaya.
Rahul: Pooja, in freedom fighters ki kahaaniyaan sunkar mujhe bahut garv mahsoos hota hai. Humare azaadi ke sapne ko poora karne ke liye humein unki yaadon ko zinda rakhte rehna chahiye.
Pooja: Haan, Rahul! Hum in mahan vyaktiyon ki kadar karte hain aur unki pratima ko samman dete hain. Woh hamare itihaas ka hissa hai.
Rahul: Sach hai, Pooja! Aur tumhara gyaan aur samajh mere dil ko choo gaya hai. Tum bhi bahut special ho, jaise ye freedom fighters. 
Pooja: Rahul, tumne mere dil ko choo liya. Tum bhi bahut achhe ho, jaise un veeron ki kathaayein. 
Rahul: Pooja, kya tumhara dil kehti hai ki hum dono bhi ek special katha banaye? Ek nayi azadi ki kahaani?
Pooja: Rahul, tumhara yeh sawaal mere dil ko chhoo gaya hai. Haan, hum dono ek special katha banayenge, jahan pyaar aur azaadi hamesha hoga.
