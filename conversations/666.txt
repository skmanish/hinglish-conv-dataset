Raj: Hey Priya, tu sirf social media ke baare mein itna passionate kyun hai?
Priya: Hey Raj, mujhe lagta hai ki social media ne news industry mein bahut bada revolution laaya hai. Log abhi apni opinions aur news articles easily share kar sakte hain.
Raj: Haan, bilkul sahi keh rahi hai tu. Par kitni reliable hai yeh social media news? Kuch rumors toh phaila rahe hain log bahut jaldi.
Priya: Haan, yeh ek badi problem hai. Log bina fact-check kiye forward kar dete hain. Lekin humara responsibility hai sahi aur credible news share karna.
Raj: Tujhe lagta hai traditional news outlets ki value kam ho gayi hai?
Priya: Nahi, traditional news outlets ka mahtva kisi se kam nahi ho gaya hai. Lekin social media ne news ko aur accessible banaya hai, especially young generation ke liye.
Raj: Achha, tu khud kya prefer karti hai? Social media ya traditional news?
Priya: Well, mujhe dono ka balance maintain karna important lagta hai. Social media keeps me updated instantly, par main always credible news sources pe rely karti hoon.
Raj: That sounds smart. Lekin kya tu kabhi fake news pe believe kar leti hai?
Priya: Haan, kabhi-kabhi ho jaata hai. But jab hamein awareness hoti hai, tab hum log easily differentiate kar sakte hain.
Raj: Tujhe kya lagta hai social media ke impact se journalism change ho gayi hai?
Priya: Definitely, journalism ko adapt hona pada hai. Ab log news ko padhna ke bajaye videos dekhna ya podcasts sunna pasand karte hain. Information ki delivery style bhi evolve ho gayi hai.
Raj: Hmmm, mujhe bhi lagta hai ki journalism mein flexibility aa gayi hai. Lekin kya yeh sach hai ki social media pe manipulative campaigns bhi chal rahi hain?
Priya: Haan, unfortunately, hai. Political parties, companies aur individuals apne interests ke liye social media ka istemal kar rahe hain. Lekin hume cautious rehna chahiye aur sahi information ke liye verify karna chahiye.
Raj: Tere saath discussion karte-karte mujhe pata chal raha hai ki tu bahut sahi sochti hai. Mujhe tujhme aur depth dikhai de rahi hai.
Priya: Thanks, Raj! Tujhse bhi bahut interesting baatein ho rahi hai. Tune bhi journalism ke impact ko bahut achhe se samjha hai.
Raj: Achha, ek aur cheez poochni thi tujhse. Kya hum kal coffee peene ja sakte hain, aur aur yeh conversation continue kar sakte hain?
