Shyam: Hey Pooja, aajkal mahilaon ki bhumika par baat karni chahiye, kya tumhare pas koi vichar hai is bare mein?
Pooja: Haan Shyam, mahilaon ka yogdan samaj mein bahut mahatvapurna hai. Wah nahi sirf ghar sambhalne mein mahir hoti hai, balki har kshetra mein kamyaabi paane mein bhi.
Shyam: Haan bilkul, Pooja. Mahilaon ne har samay apna darja sabit kiya hai, shaan se aur himmat se. Aur aajkal toh har jagah unki upasthiti dikhayi deti hai.
Pooja: Yeh bilkul sach hai, Shyam. Mahilaon ki kaamyaabi ka ek accha udaharan hai sports, jaise ki humari Mary Kom ya Saina Nehwal. Woh ladkiyan hum sabko garv ka anubhav karati hai.
Shyam: Haan, Pooja. Aur mahilaon ka yogdan politics mein bhi kafi mahatvapurna hai. Humare desh mein kuch aise mahan neta hai, jaise ki Indira Gandhi aur Pratibha Patil, jinhone woh sabit kiya ki mahila bhi desh ko sambhal sakti hai.
Pooja: Sach kaha, Shyam. Lekin abhi bhi hamare samaj mein kuch log mahilao ko kamzor samajhne lagte hai. Humein is stereotype soch se toh nikalna hoga.
Shyam: Tum bilkul sahi keh rahi ho, Pooja. Sabko samajhna chahiye ki mahilaon ka yogdan sirf ghar ke kaam mein hi nahi hai, woh har jagah apna dam dikha sakti hai.
Pooja: Haan, Shyam. Hum sabko mil kar iss stereotype soch ko badalna hoga. Aur ek tareeka hai ki hum apne ghar mein se hi shuruat karein, apne betiyo ko samjhayen ki woh bhi kuch bada kar sakti hai.
Shyam: Tumhari baat mujhe bahut pasand aayi, Pooja. Tum aise hi samaj ko badalne ke liye mahilaon ko prerit kar sakti ho.
Pooja: Dhanyavad, Shyam. Tum bhi iss badlav mein mujhe saath doge na?
Shyam: Haan, zaroor Pooja. Main hamesha tumhare saath khada rahunga. Tumhari himmat aur sahas mujhe hamesha prerna dete hain.
Pooja: Shyam, tumhara saath mere liye bahut mayne rakhta hai. Tumse baat karke mujhe acha lagta hai.
Shyam: Mujhe bhi, Pooja. Tumhari awaz sunkar, mujhe ek alag si khushi milti hai.
Pooja: Shyam, kya hum iss kutumbhit vishay pe kisi aur din bhi baat kar sakte hain?
Shyam: Zaroor, Pooja. Main hamesha tumhare saath iss tarah ke samajik mudde pe baat karne ko taiyaar rahunga.
Pooja: Shyam, shayad abhi humein thoda sannata chahiye. Tum mere saath chaloge?
Shyam: Zaroor, Pooja. Main hamesha tumhara saath dunga.
