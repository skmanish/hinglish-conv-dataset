Aryan: Meera, tumhari baat se bilkul sehmat hoon. Aur trunk calls! Remember when we used to send those written messages through train telegraph?
Meera: Aryan, woh lamhe kabhi bhool gayi? Those old-fashioned ways of communication had their own charm and excitement.
Aryan: Pehle train ki hawa kholti thi, aur sab ke saath conversations hoti thi. Aaj kal toh ek dusre se phone par baat kar lete hain, par woh jaadu aaj bhi wahi hai.

Meera: Aryan, yeh kya baat hui? Tum bhi na, kuch bhi bol dete ho!

Aryan: Meera, humari prem kahani ka safar hamesha train ke saath hi chalta rahega. Train se judi yeh yaadein humesha yaad rahengi.
