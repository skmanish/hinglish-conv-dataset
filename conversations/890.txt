Rahul: Hey Simran, kya hal hai? Monsoon ke baare mein kuchh baat karein?
Simran: Hey Rahul! Haan, monsoon pe baat karne mein maza aata hai. Is baar monsoon aapke aur aapke parivaar ke liye kaisa hai?
Rahul: Thik hai yaar, par aapke baare mein suna hai ki aapka gaon monsoon ke karan pareshaani mein hai. Sach hai?
Simran: Haan, Rahul. Is saal monsoon bohot kam tha hamare gaon mein. Crops ko bohot nuksaan hua hai. Kuchh sawaal ho toh puchh sakte ho.
Rahul: Ohh, yeh toh achha nahi hai. Crops ko paani ki bahut zarurat hoti hai. Kya sarkaar ne koi madad ki hai aapke gaon ke liye?
Simran: Haan, sarkaar ne kuchh madad ki hai. Lekin phir bhi samasya door nahi hui. Kuchh aur upaay nikalne honge.
Rahul: Aap chinta mat karo, Simran. Main hoon na, hum saath milkar upaay dhundenge. Main aapke gaon aane ki tayyari kar raha hoon.
Simran: Sach mein? Tum mere gaon aaoge? Yeh bohot achhi baat hai, Rahul. Tumhari madad se shayad hamare sajaaye gaye fields ko bacha sakein.
Rahul: Haan, Simran. Tumhari madad ke liye main hamesha tayaar hoon. Hum milkar samasyaon ka hal nikalenge. Aur phir hum dono bhi saath mein khush rahenge.
Simran: Tumhare saath mil kar saari pareshaaniyaan kam ho jati hai, Rahul. Tum mere liye special ho, aur main hamesha tumhara saath dungi.
Rahul: Tum bhi mere liye khaas ho, Simran. Tumhari muskurahat mein mujhe khushi milti hai. Main hamesha tumhare paas rahunga.
Simran: Aww, Rahul, tumhare words mere dil ko chhoo gaye. Tumhare bina zindagi adhoori hai. Tumhari yaadon mein rehna chahti hoon.
Rahul: Simran, mere saath saath chalo, hum saath mein duniya jeeyenge. Tumhari khushi meri khushi, tumhari udasi meri udasi.
Simran: Rahul, tum mere sapno ka rajkumar ho, mere zindagi ka saathi. Main tumhara saath kabhi nahi chhodungi, yeh mera vaada hai.
