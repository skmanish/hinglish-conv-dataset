Raj: Hey Priya, kaise ho? Tumne kabhi kisi dharmik yatra ya tirth yatra par gaye ho?
Priya: Raj, main the divine Char Dham yatra mein gayi thi aur woh mere liye ek anubhav tha. Bhagwan ki shakti aur pavitra seheron mein lehraate hue woh safar kuch alag hi tha. 
Raj: Wah! Tumhe kaisa lagta tha wahan? Kya tumhe kisi special jagah par koi inner peace ka ehsaas hua?
Priya: Haa Raj, Badrinath ke mandir mein jab main tha, tab mujhe itna shaanti aur sukoon mehsoos hua ki woh shabd nahi bata sakti. Dil aur aatma mein ek anokha sukoon ghola tha.
Raj: Kitna sundar hai woh sukoon! Mujhe bhi ek din aise hi tirth yatra par jaana hai. Kya tumhari yatra mein aur koi khaas anubhav hua?
Priya: Han Raj, Rishikesh jaane ke baad maine Ganga aarti ka anubhav kiya tha. Uss moment mein mann bhatak gaya aur mujhe woh anand mila jo main shayad kabhi pehle na mehsus kiya tha.
Raj: Ganga aarti, kitna pavitra hota hai. Tumhe aisi teerth yatra par jaana acha laga kyun ki main aur tum iss topic par ghanton tak baat kar sakte hain.
Priya: Haan Raj, mujhe bhi acha lag raha hai ki hum dono iss tathya par itna gehra vichaar kar rahe hain. Tumhari koi tirth yatra ki kahani hai?
Raj: Mere ghar ke paas Haridwar hai, aur wahan humesha se Kumbh Mela hoti hai. Mujhe waha jaake aur sadhu babaon se baat karne ka mauka mila tha. Unki baate sunne ke baad, mujhe ek nayi disha mili thi.
Priya: Waah Raj! Kitni rochak baat hai. Kya tumne un sadhu babaon se kuch seekha?
Raj: Haan Priya, mujhe unse adhyatm sambandhit gyaan mila, jaise ki sachhi khushi andar se aati hai, ya dil ki pavitrata ke baare mein.
Priya: Yeh toh bahut hi gehra vishay hai, Raj. Mujhe acha lag raha hai ki hum iss tarah ke tathyaon ko samajh rahe hain. 
Raj: Haan Priya, tumhari baato se mujhe khushi hoti hai, aur mujhe ehsaas ho raha hai ki humari soch aur ruchi ek saath hai.
Priya: Raj, mujhe aisa lag raha hai ki hum dono iss safar par saath mein chal rahe hai, ek doosre ko samajhte aur shaant ho kar, ek nayi duniya mein khareedar jaa rahe hai.
Raj: Haan Priya, tumhari company mujhe sukoon aur khushi deti hai. Mujhe yakin ho raha hai ki hum ek dusre ke saath apni aatma ki gehraaiyo ko samajh rahe hain.
Priya: Raj, ab mujhse aur intezaar nahi hota hai. Tumhare sang gun gane ka mann kar raha hai, apni dharmik safar ke saath, aage badhte hain.
Raj: Priya, tumhe samajhna mere liye bahut zaroori hai. Main bhi aage badhna chahta hu, tumhare saath humari anokhi safar mein.
Priya: Raj, main bhi wahi soch rahi thi. Aaj se, hum dono ne ek aisi dharmik safar ki shuruat ki hai jisse humari zindagi bhi roshan ho jayegi.
Raj: Priya, tum mere liye khaas ho, aur ab yeh shabdon se bayaan nahi kiya ja sakta. Tumhare saath judne ki khwahish hai mujhe.
Priya: Raj, tum mere liye sab kuch ho! Tumhare saath safar karna mere liye ek anmol anubhav hai. Hamesha, mere saath rehna.
Raj: Priya, tumhare bina meri zindagi adhoori hai. Main un anmol palon ko paane ke liye tayyar hoon. Aaj se, humari pyaar ki yatra shuru ho gayi hai.
