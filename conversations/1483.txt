Riya: Prateek, Ganga nadi pe kuch baat karte hai. Iski importance aur pollution issue ke baare mein. 
Prateek: Haan, bilkul! Ganga nadi hamare desh ke liye bahut hi mahatvapurna hai. Iski pavitra dhara aur sanskritik mahtvapurna batein hai.
Riya: Haan, yeh toh sahi hai. Har saal lakhon log Ganga ke kinare jaate hai iski pavitra pani mein snan karne ke liye.
Prateek: Sacchi, aur Ganga ke kinare kai dharmik sthal bhi hai, jaise Haridwar, Varanasi aur Rishikesh. Wahan ki environment aur yeh pavitra nadi, sabko shaanti aur shakti deti hai.
Riya: Lekin Prateek, mujhe dukh hota hai soch kar ke Ganga ki pavitra nadi ab pollution se bhari hui hai. Kya hum kuch nahi kar sakte?
Prateek: Riya, hum jaroor kuch kar sakte hai. Government ne bhi saari efforts shuru ki hai. Hum log bhi apne taraf se awareness spread kar sakte hai. Ghar se waste disposal sahi tareeke se karke, Ganga nadi ko pollution se bacha sakte hai.
Riya: Haan, bilkul. Sabko samjhna chahiye ki Ganga nadi hamari ma hai. Hum uski respect kaise kar sakte hai, pollution nahi faila sakte.
Prateek: Sahi keh rahi ho, Riya. Humare desh ke log bhi isko samajhna chahiye. Pollution ko kam karne ke liye, industries ko sambhalna chahiye. Aur sadko ke drainage system ko bhi improve karna chahiye.
Riya: Prateek, tumhara ye soch mujhe bahut pasand aata hai. Tum environment ke baare mein bahut aware ho.
Prateek: Riya, hum sabko sochna chahiye. Nahi toh humara future hi khatre mein hai. Ganga nadi jaisi pavitra nadi ko bachana hamara kartavya hai.
Riya: Prateek, tumhare saath baat karke bahut achha lagta hai. Humare vicharon mein bahut similarity hai.
Prateek: Haan, Riya. Tumhari baatein sun ke dil khush ho jata hai. Tumhare saath samay bitane mein mujhe bahut maza aata hai.
Riya: Prateek, mujhe bhi tumhara saath bahut acha lagta hai. Humare soch ek dusre se milte hai.
Prateek: Riya, kya tum mere saath, Ganga ke kinare yatra pe chalogi? Waha hum Ganga ki pavitra dhara mein badhte hai aur humare vicharon ka samaan karte hai.
Riya: Prateek, main bilkul chalungi. Waha samay bitane ka mauka humari dosti aur prem ko aur gehra kar dega.
Prateek: Riya, yeh sunke dil khush ho raha hai. Hamari yatra ke dauran, hum aur nazdeekiya bana sakte hai. Mujhe tumse pyaar ho gaya hai.
Riya: Prateek, mujhe bhi tumse pyaar ho gaya hai. Yatra ke dauran, humare pyaar ki pavitra dhara bahut gehri ho jayegi.
