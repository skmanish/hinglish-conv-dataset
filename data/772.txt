Rahul: Hey Simran! Kya haal hai? Kaisi ho?

Simran: Hey Rahul! Main teekh hoon. Tum kaise ho? 

Rahul: Main bhi teekh hoon, yaar. Toh, tune inn recent political events ke baare mein kya socha?

Simran: Honestly, politics is not really my cup of tea. Par kuch interesting hua kya?

Rahul: Haan, bahut interesting. BJP ne toh pehli baar itni seats jeeti hai. Kya kehti ho?

Simran: Achha? Isse alag seats jeetne ki baat sunne mein sach ya jhoot lagta hai. Par phir bhi, kisi ko pasand karne se zyada important hai ki sahi insaan jeete.

Rahul: Bilkul sahi baat hai, Simran. Lekin mujhe toh politics itni samajh nahi aati hai, bas headlines pe focus kar leta hoon.

Simran: Tumhe politics ki knowledge hona zaruri nahi hai, Rahul. Mere pass toh bas general idea hai. Par kuch important issues par baat karte hain.

Rahul: Haan, bilkul. Jaise ki, unemployment aur education reforms. Ispe kya sochti ho?

Simran: Unemployment toh sach mein bahut badi problem hai. Youth ko job opportunities chahiye. Education reforms bhi zaruri hai, quality education ke liye efforts hone chahiye.

Rahul: Absolutely, Simran. Wohi main bhi sochta hoon. Youth ke liye sahi opportunities aur quality education provide karna zaruri hai.

Simran: Rahul, mujhe achha lag raha hai ki hum political issues par discuss kar rahe hain. It feels refreshing and intellectually stimulating.

Rahul: Haan, mujhe bhi. Tumhare saath politics ke baare mein baat karke mere dil ki pumping speed badh rahi hai.

Simran: Oh really? Dil ki pumping speed, haan? Lagta hai, tumhari dil ki pumping speed politics se jyada mujhse badh gayi hai.

Rahul: (blushing) Waise Simran, tumhari smile toh dil ki pumping speed ko bhi peeche chhod deti hai.

Simran: Rahul, tum bhi kuch kam nahi ho. Tumhare baatein sunke mera dil bhi jaise kuch aur hi bol raha hai.

Rahul: (taking a deep breath) Simran, maine yeh decide kar liya hai ki tumse dil ki pumping speed saath hi apna dil bhi share karna chahta hoon. Mujhe tumse pyar ho gaya hai.

Simran: Rahul, tumse pyar karne ke liye dil ki pumping speed ki zarurat nahi padti hai. Kyunki main bhi tumse pyar karti hoon.

(They both share a tender moment and hold hands)
