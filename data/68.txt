Riya: Sat shri akal, Raj! Kiven aa tu?

Raj: Sat shri akal, Riya! Main chang aa, yaar. Tusi ki haal hai?

Riya: Mai vi chang aa, Raj. Suneya tusi Punjab de farmer ho. Agriculture laws bare ki sochde ho?

Raj: Haan, Riya. Eh nehle time vaddi controversy hai. Punjab de farmera nu bahut problem horahi hai.

Riya: Oh haan, main vi suneya. Ki problems aa rahi ne?

Raj: Bai, government ne keha ne kisanan de liye benefits honge, par eh sab kuch income loss te debt vich fans jan da dar hai.

Riya: Hor ki hor badlav chahide ne farmers?

Raj: Pehlan, sarkar di guarantee chahidi hai ki sade arhtiyas sahi tareeqe naal payment karan. Hun ohna nu cash payment de naal replace karna chahida hai.

Riya: Haan, oh vi sahi hai. Make it more transparent and eradicate the middlemen.

Raj: Viakhri saal di faslon da MSP vi maintain honi chahidi hai, taaki farmers di kamai vaddi rahe.

Riya: Eh sahi hai, farmers di income nu protect karna zaroori hai. Satkaran deviy
