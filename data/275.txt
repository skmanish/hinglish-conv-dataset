Riya: Koi shak nahi. Yahaan ki shanti aur sukoon jindagi mein waapas lana chahti hu.
Rahul: Riya, hum dono yahaan itni khubsurat jagah par, ek saath, itna nazdeek... dil toh machal raha hai, yaar!

Rahul: Riya, jab tu hashti hai, toh itni khoobsurat lagti hai. Tera chehra inn backwaters ki sundarta se bhi hardhaf ho jaata hai.

Riya: Rahul, tu shayari bhi karta hai? Kitna sweet hai! Tumse baat karte karte, mere dil mein pyaar ki bijliya chamak rahi hai.
Rahul: Riya, tu meri dhadkan hai. Tujhe khona nahi chahta hu. Sach kahun toh, tujhse pyaar karne ka mann kar raha hai.
Riya: Rahul, hum yahaan pe hai, door kisi duniya se. Is mauke ko hum saath mein jeena chahte hai, yaar.
Rahul: Riya, yeh mauka humari kismat ka tohfa hai. Main tujhe khona nahi chahta hu, kyun ki tere bina yahaan ki khubsurati adhuri hai.

Riya: Rahul, yeh jagah itni pyaari hai humare liye. Yeh humari shuruaat hai, saath mein pyaar ki nayi kahani likhte hai.
Rahul: Riya, yeh backwaters humare pyaar ki nishaani hai. Main tujhse pyaar karta hu aur hamesha karta rahunga.
Riya: Rahul, yeh pal humesha yaad rahenge. Tumhare saath bitaye din mere dil mein hamesha liye baste rahenge.
Rahul: Riya, tere saath bitaye lamhe mere liye anmol hai. Kabhi bhi khona nahi chahta hu.
Riya: Rahul, main bhi hamesha tere saath rehna chahti hu. Tum mere sapnon ki duniya ho.
Rahul: Riya, tu meri khushi hai. Tere saath bitaye pal mujhe zindagi ka ehsaas karate hai.
Riya: Rahul, ek promise karo. Kabhi yeh pyaar na jaye, aur humesha saath mein rahe.
Rahul: Riya, main hamesha teri saath rahunga, pyaar se bhare iss safar mein. Tujhe kabhi bhi khona nahi chahta hu.
Riya: Rahul, tere bina yeh safar adhura hai. Tu mere saath hamesha yahaan rehna.
Rahul: Riya, hum yahaan backwaters ke saath saath, apne pyaar ki kashti banate hai. Tum meri zindagi ke hote hue, zindagi ki sabse khoobsurat jagah ban gayi ho.
