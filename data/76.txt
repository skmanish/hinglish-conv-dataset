Sid: Hi Riya, kaisi ho? Kuch time ke liye Kolkata me ho, right?
Riya: Haan, Sid! Rabindranath Tagore ke saath modern literature ke baare mein baat karne aaye hain. Tumhe kuch pata hai uske baare mein?
Sid: Are, Tagore ho yaar! Woh thay ek kamaal ke shayar aur lekhak. Unki kavita ko padh ke, dil khush ho jata hai.
Riya: Haan yaar, unki kavita ka andaaz alag tha, itna deep aur profound. Unhone toh emotions ki duniya ko choo liya tha.
Sid: Bilkul, Riya! Unhone iss desh ke logon tak kavitayen pahuncha di aur unki soch ko badla. Unki anubhutiyan aj bhi hamari zindagi me jiwit hai.
Riya: Poetries padhne se dil ko sukoon milta hai, na? Unki kavita me ek prem aur samvedna hai, jo insaan ko hamesha yaad rehti hai.
Sid: Sahi baat hai, Riya! Unhone naye tajurbe aur rishton ki bhasha ko samjha. Waise, tumhe kaunsi Tagore ki kavita sabse zyada pasand hai?
Riya: Sid, mujhe 'Gitanjali' bahut acchi lagti hai. Usme woh bharat ki takat, mohabbat aur vishwas ko aise roop me chitrit kar diya hai jo aaj bhi hamein prerna deta hai.
Sid: Wow, Riya, tumhe lafzon ki taqat ko samajhna aata hai. Tumhare andaaz me ek khubsurat kavita likh sakti ho, aakhir tumhe Tagore ki kitni kavitayen aati hai?
Riya: Sid, tumhara pyaar mere liye shayri ke tohfe ke baraabar hai. Shayari mujhe aati hai, par Tagore jaise shayar nahi ban sakti. Tum samajhoge ya nahi?
Sid: Riya, sach bolu toh, tumhare andar woh kashish hai, woh roshni hai jo mujhe khiche hai. Tumhari aankhon mein bhi kahaniyan hai, jo mujhe har roz bataati hain.
Riya: Sid, tum bhi toh kisi sheher ke kavi ki tarah hote ho. Tumhare alfazon se meri duniya sajti hai aur mere dil ko sukoon milta hai.
Sid: Riya, tumhari muskurahat aur andaaz ne mujhe apni taraf kheecha hai. Tumhare saath rehkar, jahan bhi ho, woh jagah mere liye khuda ki ek jannat ban gayi hai.
Riya: Sid, tumhare saath waqt bitana, kavita padhna aur tumhare saath Rabindranath Tagore ke baare mein baat karna, yeh mere liye khushnuma anubhav hai.
Sid: Riya, tumhari khushi aur muskurahat mujhe jeevan bhar yaad rahegi. Mujhe yakeen hai ki hum dono ki kahani bhi wohi khoobsurat hongi jaise Tagore ki kavitayen.
Riya: Sid, tumhare hone se meri duniya me roshni aa gayi hai. Tumhare saath rehkar har lamha mere liye sabse special ban gaya hai.
Sid: Riya, jab se tum Kolkata mein aayi ho, meri zindagi me ek nayi khushi ka rang bhar gaya hai. Chalo, yahan Rabindra Sarobar ke kinare chalte hain, wahan apni duniya basaenge.
Riya: Sid, tum mere saath ho, toh har jagah rangin aur pyaara lagta hai. Chalo, hum Rabindra Sarobar mei bhare huye kaviyon ke jahan ko apna banate hai.
(They both walk hand in hand towards the beautiful lakeside, lost in their own world, as the sun sets and the romance fills the air.)
